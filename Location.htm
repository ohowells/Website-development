Where are we?


Our campuses are student communities with a great atmosphere. On campus,

you’ll find everything you need in one place – your classes, the library,

places to eat and drink, and friends to spend time with.


The University of south wales has five main campuses:


  • Treforest campus
  • Glyntaff campus
  • Cardiff campus
  • Caerleon campus
  • Newport City campus
